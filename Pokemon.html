Introduction

Its a japanese media franchise managed by Pokemon Company, it was founded by Nintendo, Gmae Freak and Creatures - all three of them are japanese companies.
The franchise was originally created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1996, he originally made the game from his hobby of collecting insects, he originally came up with this idea in 1990 after seeing the Game Boy which was made by nintendo and the ability to communicate between them via the cable, he pitched the idea to the Nintendo who you didn't really grasp the idea but were they decided to explore the concept as Satoshi had a good reputation for game desing, it took 6 years to make the first two game's in the franchise they were named Pokemon red and Pokemon blue, in the process of development, Game Freak nearly went bankrupt to develop the game luckily Creature INC invested into the game in return they would receive one-third of the franchise right.
Very few media payed attention to the franchise as such Satoshi feared that Nintendo would reject the games, but over time the sales were increased until, the franchise found itself as one of the best selling games among Nintendo's top franchises such as Mario, he would continue being involved in the more modern Pokemon Games as well.
Satoshi saw Shigeru Miyamoto as a major influence, because of this he develpoed a style closely to his, he grew inspiration from olf Japanese shows and anime such as Godzilla and Ultraman.
